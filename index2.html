Structure

The abstract understanding for this is not about an abstract arrangement, but The abstract on this is about a beginning of the story that want to be told on the next story(Like in the orientation,etc).

Orientation has a relationship with time, atmosphere, place in the short story. This is what makes a short story more interesting because it uses an interesting background.

The structure of the next short story text is a complication. Understanding complications in the short story is a sequence of events that are linked by cause and effect. Characters and character are usually seen in the structure of this complication that illustrates the plot of the story.

Evaluation becomes the next type of short story structure. Understanding the evaluation of the short story is a conflict that occurs and leads to a climax. In the evaluation, the conflict has started to get solutions and solutions and is going to the final stage.

Resolution is one of the short text structure. The definition of resolution in a short story is when the author expresses a solution to the problems experienced by a character in a short story. In the resolution, the problem has been solved at the end of the story.

The last structure is coda,Coda in the short story is a value or lesson that can be obtained from the short story text by the reader.Usually the message is a good thing and can be emulated in everyday life
